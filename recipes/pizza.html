Pizza

Description

Sometimes you want to go big and go home. That’s where this supreme pizza recipe comes in. Made with all the classic toppings right in your own oven, it’s indulgent-feeling while still managing some balance and restraint.

Ingredients

  • Thick Marinara Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Ham
  • Parmesan
  • Pizza Dough
  • Sausage
  • Cornmeal
  • Mozzarella
  • Cremini Mushrooms
  • Black Olives
  • Green Peppers
  • Red Onions

Steps

  1. Start with homemade dough, if you like (or not! no judgment)
  2. Spread on some thick, super-flavorful sauce
  3. Sprinkle on the perfect blend of cheeses
  4. Add meat meat meat and razor-thin veggies
  5. Bake to perfection and serve with minimal fuss
