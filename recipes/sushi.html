Sushi Recipe

Description

Homemade Sushi is so much cheaper than at the restaurant. Plus, it’s easy and fun to make your own rolls at home, so you can put all your favorite ingredients into your perfect custom roll — here’s how!

Ingredients

  • Medium Grain Rice
  • Cold Water
  • Sushi Vinegar
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Imitation Crab
  • Avocado
  • Medium Cucumber
  • Nori Seaweed
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Sushi Rolling Mat

Steps

  1. Wash rice with cold water.
  2. Cook rice then dry.
  3. Fold pieces of nori in half and split
  4. Toast sesame seeds
  5. Spread generous amount of rice onto nori.
  6. Flip nori with rice upside down and place remaining ingredients.
  7. Roll nori into a Sushi Roll.
  8. Cut sushi into smaller slices and enjoy!
